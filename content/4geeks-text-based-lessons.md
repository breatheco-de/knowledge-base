# Text-Based Lessons

With the internet giving us so much information, sometimes it's hard to know what's accurate. This info overload can be confusing, especially if we're new to something. Because of this, text-based lessons tend to be an useful tool for many successful online learning platforms, and for good reason. They provide a structured, in-depth, and flexible approach to learning that accommodates a wide range of learners. Especially in programming, topics can become quite large and complex.
